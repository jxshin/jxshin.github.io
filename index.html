Welcome to my home page!

I am a Ph.D. student in the Institute of Software,School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University, P. R. China. I received my Bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2011.

I'm interested in mining software repositories to understand technical and social aspects of software development.
